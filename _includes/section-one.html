What we do

Knowmore works with nonprofits and other mission-driven organizations both big and small to enable them to use data to drive their decision making. Many in these industries are not taking full advantage of data science because 1: they don't understand it fully, and 2: they don't have the resources. By connecting data scientists and engineers with socially impactful projects, we provide opportunities to leverage the power of data towards bettering humanity.
